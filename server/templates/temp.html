Home Page

This project is primarily concerned with being able to classify patients on whether or not they have hepatitis C based on a variety of different patient data.

It is estimated that yearly around 15000 Americans die annually form Hepatitis C and worldwide, that figure is around 290000. Most deaths occur from liver cirrhosis and liver cancer, so our study aims to classify on multiple types of Hepatitis C categorisations.
